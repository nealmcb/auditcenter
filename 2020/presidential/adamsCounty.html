Adams County

< Back to Audit Center

Adams County closed their government offices on Thursday, March 12th to prevent the spread of COVID-19. The 2020 Presidential Primary Election RLA commenced on Friday, March 13th, with Adams County excluded as they were unable to participate in the statewide audit due to their offices being shut down. Adams County worked with the Colorado Secretary of State’s office to create a plan which enabled them to audit the target contests within the Adams County universe of ballots, and separate from the already completed statewide audit. Adams County finished their audit on Tuesday, March 24th, with no discrepancies.
